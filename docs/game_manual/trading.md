# Trading

Players can send resources to other players and hire caravans for goods.

## Send Menu

> "Send Menu — this button takes the player to a submenu where the player can selectively send various resources (gold, items, lands) to other players. In short — a trading button."
> — Help file, line 157

The Send Menu allows you to transfer resources to other players:

### Sendable Resources

| Resource | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Gold | Must have more gold than minimum threshold |
| Items | Must have at least 1 item in inventory |
| Lands | Must own at least 1 land |

---

## Sending Gold

You can send gold directly to another player:
1. Open Send Menu
2. Select "Send Gold"
3. Choose target player
4. Specify amount
5. Confirm transfer

The gold is immediately transferred to the target player.

---

## Sending Items

You can send items from your inventory to another player:
1. Open Send Menu
2. Select "Send Item"
3. Choose target player
4. Select item from your inventory
5. Confirm transfer

The item moves from your inventory to the target player's inventory.

---

## Sending Lands

You can transfer ownership of lands you own to another player:
1. Open Send Menu
2. Select "Send Land"
3. Choose target player
4. Select which land(s) to send
5. Confirm transfer

**Note:** You can send all lands of a specific type at once, or send individual lands.

When a land is transferred:
- Ownership changes to the target player
- Any buildings on the land remain
- Fortifications and defenders transfer with the land

---

## Caravans (Optional Feature)

Caravans are an optional game feature available at the Bazaar. When enabled, you can hire caravans that will bring goods to you.

### How Caravans Work

1. Visit a Bazaar
2. If caravans are enabled, you can hire one
3. Pay the caravan cost
4. The caravan travels and returns with goods
5. Collect your goods at any Bazaar

### Caravan Types

| Caravan | Returns |
|---------|---------|
| Small Caravan | 1 random item (value 80–401 gold) |
| Large Caravan | 2–4 random items (variable value) |

**Large Caravan Details:**
The large caravan's return is randomized:
- Chance 1: 4 items (value 80–401 each)
- Chance 2: 3 items (value 150–600 each)
- Chance 3: 2 items (value 200–1000 each)

**Note:** Caravan costs are configurable game options.

---

## Trading Summary

| Action | Cost | Effect |
|--------|------|--------|
| Send Gold | Free | Transfer gold to player |
| Send Item | Free | Transfer item to player |
| Send Land | Free | Transfer land ownership |
| Small Caravan | Variable | Receive 1 item (80–401 gold value) |
| Large Caravan | Variable | Receive 2–4 items (variable value) |

---

## Notes

- Trading requires you to be in a location where the Send Menu is available
- You cannot send resources to yourself
- Caravans are an optional feature that may not be enabled in all games
- Caravan goods are collected at Bazaars
